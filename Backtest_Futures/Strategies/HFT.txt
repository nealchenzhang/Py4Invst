High-frequency traders
(a) require a high-speed trading infrastructure,
(b) have investment time horizons less than one day, and
(c) generally try to end the day with no positions whatsoever.

Categories:
1) UHFTs - ultra-high-frequency traders,
2) HFTs,
3) MFTs -  medium-frequency traders, and
4) Algorithmic execution engines.

Trading Orders:
a) Aggressive Orders:
    Market Orders
    Limit Order at best counter-party price
b) Passive Orders:
    Limit Orders that cannot be immediately filled

Liquidity:
    immediate availability of units to be transacted at a fair price
